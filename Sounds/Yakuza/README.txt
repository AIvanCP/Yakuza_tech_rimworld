This folder contains sound effects for Yakuza Combat techniques.

Expected sound files:
- TigerDrop.wav or TigerDrop.ogg
- KomakiParry.wav or KomakiParry.ogg  
- HeatSpin.wav or HeatSpin.ogg
- FirearmCounter.wav or FirearmCounter.ogg

Supported formats: .wav, .ogg

Place your custom sound files here and they will be automatically played when techniques trigger.
